Project Overview:

Visually impaired individuals often have difficulties with navigating indoors and can feel more uncomfortable and unaware of their surroundings. BlindPath aims to help combat this struggle by assisting UTD students with navigating to their classes. BlindPath will safely guide the user to their destination on time and comfortably.
